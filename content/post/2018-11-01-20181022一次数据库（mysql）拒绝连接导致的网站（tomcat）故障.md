中午接到反馈，某个web出现了打不开的状况。

&nbsp;

自己打开试一下，果然不行。

&nbsp;

浏览器F12调试发现反向代理的nginx报了404的响应头，这说明另一台主机上被代理的Tomcat应该是还在运行的，否则nginx就会报502了。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-359" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125341.png" alt="" width="2568" height="1408" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125341.png 2568w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125341-300x164.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125341-768x421.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125341-1024x561.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2568px) 100vw, 2568px" />

&nbsp;

直接访问被反向代理的主机试一下，返回的状态码还是404。

<img class="alignnone wp-image-360 size-full" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125413-e1541064785251.png" alt="" width="2568" height="1348" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125413-e1541064785251.png 2568w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125413-e1541064785251-300x157.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125413-e1541064785251-768x403.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125413-e1541064785251-1024x538.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2568px) 100vw, 2568px" />

&nbsp;

看一下tomcat的stdout日志，这一堆报错可把我这个java业余人士愁的头都大了。不过看起来似乎是在报jdbc超时，初步怀疑数据库出了问题。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-361" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125435.png" alt="" width="2560" height="1440" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125435.png 2560w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125435-300x169.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125435-768x432.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125435-1024x576.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />

&nbsp;

不过我觉得数据库不大可能出问题，毕竟在同一个实例里面的其他项目都还正常运行。

再看下tomcat的站点日志，日志里面提到了quartz-task.xml这么一个文件。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-362" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125452.png" alt="" width="2560" height="1440" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125452.png 2560w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125452-300x169.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125452-768x432.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125452-1024x576.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />

&nbsp;

打开看看，这个文件居然是数据库调取器的配置文件之类的东西。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-363" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125459.png" alt="" width="2560" height="1440" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125459.png 2560w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125459-300x169.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125459-768x432.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125459-1024x576.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />

&nbsp;

得了，那就在这台主机上连接一下数据库试试吧。

还真别说，使用navicat连接的时候报了1129这个错误，这台主机出现了太多的连接错误所以被主机给block了。

<img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-364" src="https://blog.tracewalker.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125514.png" alt="" width="2560" height="1440" srcset="/images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125514.png 2560w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125514-300x169.png 300w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125514-768x432.png 768w, /images/2018/11/QQ截图20181022125514-1024x576.png 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 2560px) 100vw, 2560px" />

按照提示在数据库主机执行`mysqladmin flush-hosts`

然后重启tomcat。

再次访问网站，成功打开。
